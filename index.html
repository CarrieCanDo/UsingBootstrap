About Oceans 4 All

Here at Oceans 4 All, we are a local non-profit who works together with local businesses and citizens. Together, we organize events to raise funds to provide beach parking passes for those who cannot afford to pay to go to the beach. We believe that all our citizens should have access to the local beaches as it is not only good for your health but also a great educational experience for our young people. We appreciate all who contribute to these efforts. If you are interested in volunteering or contributing in another way, please contact us.
